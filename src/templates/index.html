Bedside Template Designer

Print-and-laminate delirium reference sheets, customized to your unit

Adult tool Pediatric tool
Reference aid only. These sheets support — and do not replace — clinical judgment, local protocol, and prescriber/pharmacy review. They are not a validated decision-support device or an order set. Verify all medication content against your formulary before use.

Pick a template, switch sections and medications on or off, add your unit's own protocol lines, then print at 100% on Letter paper and laminate — staff mark the boxes with a dry-erase pen at the bedside. Your customization stays on this device and can be shared with colleagues as a link.
